Mighty Kit System

Our patented and certified Mighty Kit System (MKS) made out of our 3D printed panels are unlike any other wall system in the industry. Each panel uses a revolutionary non-silicate Light Stone Material and steel components. This building material provides a high-performance, sustainable, durable, and cost-efficient solution for construction across the United States.

The one of a kind Mighty Kit System makes transporting large units simple from creation to installation. On-site installation has never been easier or more affordable.

Advantages of 3D Printed Panels:

Quick Assembly & Low Maintenance

A single composite panel replaces up to eight layers of traditional construction materials and associated trade coordination.

Durability

Our stone-like material is combined with steel, so it will last for decades and is 100% compliant for fire resistance.

High Quality Results

80% of our production is automated in a controlled environment, limiting issues related to typical site-built projects.

High thermal resistance

Using high density insulation provides energy savings and compliance with the most strict energy codes.

Price Predictability

Fabrication prices are easily assessed and can be locked in.

Accessible on Nearly Every Site

Our team is able to install one of our units on a diverse range of properties and lots.

Sustainability

By printing only what we need, we can produce units with virtually zero waste — eliminating the 3 - 5 lbs of waste per sq. ft. generated by traditional home construction.

Compliance

This panelized system is already compliant with California building codes and quality standards.

Why Mighty Kit System?

  • All construction is completed in our factory which translates into a massive reduction in construction soft costs and reduced on-site labor hours (faster schedule & increased IRR).
  • Architecture is pre-approved at the state level via Housing Community Development (HCD) Factory Built Housing program (FBH). City architectural review is limited to site plan layout and unit attachment to foundation.
  • Factory built housing allows developers to achieve large production housing economies of scale and volume discounts.
  • Near-zero waste technology (savings of 2.3 tons of CO2 emission per 3D printed home). Insurance premiums are generally 10% lower (homes are constructed with non-combustible materials).
  • Ability to partner with land holders at the equity level and or provide flexible payment terms once the unit is delivered.

MIGHTY PANEL

3D PRINTED POLYMER COMPOSITE PANEL

Arrives as 100% complete panel: structure, insulation, MEP integration, air/moisture/fire barrier & interior/exterior finishes. Supports digital fabrication platform from design through build.

WOOD STUD FRAMED PANEL

  • High thermal bridging of wood structural frame
  • Requires many material layers of build-up
  • Panels are heavy & MEP integration is challenging
  • Finishes typically not included, and if so, require significant on-site seaming at joints

CONCRETE 3D PRINTED PANEL

  • Concrete has poor thermal performance
  • Thick, inconsistent print layers not trimmable
  • 3Dp concrete is slow due to cure times (stop/start)
  • Reinforcement is not easily automated (steel/fiber)
  • Can only 3D print walls (no floor or roof)

STRUCTURAL INSULATED PANEL (SIP)

  • OSB SIPS do not include wrap or int/ext finishes
  • Difficult to integrate structured MEPs through foam
  • Limited customization options with fixed SIPs
  • Niche product due to cost greater than traditional wood stud wall with batt insulation

Light Stone Material

Mighty Buildings uses 3D printing technology and proprietary Light Stone Material (LSM) as an alternative to conventional building materials. Our breakthrough LSM can be printed in any shape and hardens to a stone-like composite.

This award-winning material enables a near-zero waste production process and is more durable and energy efficient without the need for additional insulation, saving developers and homeowners money from the start. Using a 3D printing process called Photo-Activated Component Extrustion, rapid curing allows the LSM to print in horizontal layers without a supporting structure, giving our Mighty Buildings sturdy 3D printed walls, floors, ceilings and more.

By building our Mighty units with our innovative Light Stone Material, we offer durable, sustainable houses and ADUs that are as versatile as they are stylish.
