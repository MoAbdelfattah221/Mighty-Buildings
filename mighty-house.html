Mighty Quatro

The Mighty Quatro has an open-concept layout and spacious kitchen island that makes it perfect for hosting friends and family. Entertain guests around the kitchen island while enjoying the natural light streaming in through floor-to-ceiling windows. The extra bedroom can serve as a guest room for visitors wishing to stay the night. By giving you more space and more options, the Mighty Quatro offers you the freedom to create a home that is uniquely yours.

2 Beds / 2 Baths, 1176 sq. ft.

The fully-equipped kitchen offers a full-sized refrigerator, custom-made cabinets, a four-burner range, dishwasher, and dual-purpose convection oven and microwave.

Ample built-in storage space is available throughout the home, including in the hallway, closets, bedrooms, and baths.

The luxury bath features quartz countertops, an elegant mirrored medicine cabinet and vanity, and a walk-in shower with designer-selected fixtures. The Mighty Quatro also includes a washer and dryer.
